# Automate Your Inventory Management: Introducing the Cin7 Core Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the Cin7 Core (DEAR Inventory) integration. As inventory management becomes increasingly complex across multiple sales channels, automation isn't just nice to have—it's essential.

## The Challenge of Omnichannel Inventory

Businesses today sell everywhere: online stores, marketplaces, brick-and-mortar locations, and wholesale channels. Managing inventory, orders, and purchasing across these channels manually leads to stock discrepancies, delayed orders, and frustrated customers. Cin7 Core is a powerful inventory management platform, but its true potential is unlocked when it seamlessly connects with your other business systems.

## Enter the n8n-nodes-cin7-core

Velocity BPA has developed this community node to bridge Cin7 Core with the n8n automation ecosystem. Now you can create sophisticated workflows that connect your inventory management with CRM systems, accounting software, e-commerce platforms, notification tools, and hundreds of other applications.

## Key Capabilities

This node enables you to:
- Sync inventory levels across multiple sales channels in real-time
- Automatically create purchase orders when stock reaches reorder points
- Push sales data to your accounting or analytics platforms
- Send notifications when orders are fulfilled or shipments are dispatched
- Update customer records when purchases are made
- Generate reports by pulling data from Cin7 Core and combining it with other sources

## Getting Started

Installing the Cin7 Core node is straightforward. In your n8n instance, simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-cin7-core


After installation, restart n8n and you'll find the Cin7 Core node available in your node palette. Configure your API credentials, and you're ready to start building powerful inventory automation workflows.
